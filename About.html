  • INDIAN CULTURAL ASSOCIATION

      Indian Cultural Association Singapore , a truely volunteer organisation was taken birth with the primary aim of Socio-cultural integration of Indian diaspora in singapore. It was conceived a decade ago, as a result of unconditional and selfless initiative by a group of working professionals. since then ICAS has been involving in various activities with full dedication

  •  “Indian Cultural Association” is a registered society under Registry of Societies Singapore which was founded in 2006.The official inauguration was done by the well-known Malayalam poet Shri Balachandran Chullikkadu in September 2006.

  •  A group of volunteers and well-wishers from South India who were active in community services formed the “Indian Cultural Association” which is a non-profit organization. There are life members and ordinary members in the Association.Indian Cultural Association is working among Singapore community with significant activities since its formation.

  •  The Association has achieved remarkable acceptance among the community through the various activities.The membership strength shows an yearly increasing trend and it’s reached 500+ in 2017 A successful 10th year celebrations has been done in the year 2016 with renowned Indian Artists.

  • Meaning & Significance of our Logo:

    Members working together to promote Indian Culture in a culturally diversified Singapore with harmony, passion and truthfulness through our 5 types of activities such as cultural, children, welfare, sports and interaction.

  •  Significance of the colours used in the proposed Symbol:

  •  1. Blue - Harmony 2. Red - Passion 3. White - Truthfulness 4. Tri Colour - Integration of culture

vision & mission

  • Creating awareness and promote the rich Indian cultural heritage among the multi ethnic Singapore community by providing opportunities to listen, share and perform Indian Arts to know the culture in a better way.
  • Actively celebrate community festivals in its traditional way to preserve the essence of the rich culture with high participation of integrated community.
  • Proactively involve and lead the activities in the society with Community Clubs and Grass roots organizations.
  • Encourage younger generation to actively participate in the yearly platform of Children Cultural fest to perform their talents and develop their skills.
  • Organize events of sports by aiming healthy life style.
  • Ensure the helping hands to the needy and provide support to manage unexpected events and other crisis solutions.
  • Create a vast network among the members and well-wishers to know each other and to get-together for cultural exchanges.
